History

Founded in 2010 by a small group of enthusiasts, TechCon has grown from a one-day regional meetup to a multi-day international conference. Over the years we expanded our program to include hands-on workshops, startup showcases, and deep-dive technical tutorials.

Each year, TechCon has adapted to the changing technology landscape — adding tracks for AI, privacy, cloud-native systems, and developer tools — while keeping community and accessibility at its core.

Mission

Our mission is to accelerate knowledge sharing and foster an inclusive community where practitioners and learners can collaborate, experiment, and build the future.

We prioritize diverse voices, practical learning, and responsible technology. Whether you are presenting research or learning a new tool, TechCon is designed to help you grow.
